Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portra of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the french Neoclasiccal artist Jean Auguste Diminique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviere family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musee du Louvre Paris
